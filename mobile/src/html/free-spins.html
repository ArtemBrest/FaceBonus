What Makes A Good Free Spin Bonus?

Turnover Conditions

This is one of the most important bonus conditions that dictates the overall value of any online casino bonus, also commonly known as the wagering requirements. Any free spin bonus with turnover conditions included in the rules will mean that you need to wager a certain amount of money before that bonus is converted into real cash money and made withdrawable.

Say if you had won €50 from your free spins and the rules stated a 20x turnover condition. You would then need to wager a total of €1,000 before you could even think about withdrawing your money. It therefore goes without saying that a free spin bonus holds a lot more value when there are lower turnover conditions, or better still, none at all.

Maximum Winnings Limit

A classic rule often hidden away in the terms and conditions is the maximum winnings limit and this is cleverly included by the casinos to give themselves a safety net. It is something that you will likely find within all no deposit free spin bonuses, and it basically means that you will only be able to win a certain amount of money from the winnings you make.

If you won €250 on a slot game using the free spins bonus, but the maximum winnings limit had been set to €50. You would only be able to withdraw €50 of that money and the other €200 would be taken back by the casino. Remember, that €50 may then also be subjected to the turnover requirements mentioned earlier. The higher the limit then the better the free spin bonus is for you, as you can potentially win more.

Expiry Dates

The free spin bonuses that give you the most time to not only enjoy the spins, but also to clear any of the given turnover conditions are the ones that you want to look out for. The bonuses you want to try and stay clear of are the 24-hour bonuses or the ones that expiry in just a couple of days. Many of the best online casinos will offer up to 30 days to enjoy and use their bonus spins, and this is considered to be a fair time-frame in our eyes.

Game Exclusion & Weighting

The exclusion of certain games is another clever ploy by the casinos to give themselves a much-needed safety net yet again. In the terms and conditions, you will find a list of games that are not eligible for use with the free spins. The reason being is that some slot games come with much higher RTP rates than others and are therefore likely to pay out more money, which may be good for you, but it is not good for the casinos.

Additionally, game weighting in the bonus conditions is another important rule to consider. Many slot games will come with 100% weighting and other tables games may have anything from 50% down to 5% and below. It means that 100% of your wagers on the eligible slot games will count towards clearing the turnover conditions, whereas only the percentage weighting of the table game will be counted.

The last thing you want is to make a deposit to claim a bundle of free spins, to only then miss out on the spins because they had a 24-hour expiry. It would essentially be a wasted deposit if it were the spins that you really wanted. Remember, it always pays to check the terms and conditions first. If you check the expiry and all the points above, then you will enjoy your free spins and your Stakersland experience a whole lot more.
