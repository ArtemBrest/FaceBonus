High RTP
Your first port of call is to check the RTP of all new online slots and try to aim for those games that offer at least 95% or higher. A helpful tip to remember is that the displayed RTP is defined as a fixed value and these are estimated on tens of millions of spins, which makes them irrelevant to your individual play. Another small but useful piece of advice for our stakers here is that some of the casinos are given the opportunity to adjust the RTPs on featured games, so always check the RTP of new online slots at each of the casinos that you play at. Also take note that new games are likely to hold higher RTPs in the early days which is a tactic used by the software providers to entice you in and play.
Variance/Volatility
Game studios are aiming to reach complete gaming liquidity and they use ever-evolving tactics by mixing pay tables and paylines with the most favourable Theoretical RTP values to achieve this. This does not always work though, and different tracking tools are used to understand churn and burn factors (the moment you give up on playing the newly released game). New online slots with high volatility usually means that you will receive occasional big wins. If you wanted a fair and balanced gaming session, then choosing a slot with medium volatility would be more enjoyable.
Slot Features
The one thing that most stakers enjoy the most about slot gaming entertainment is the immersive bonus rounds and special symbol features. The bigger studios even go as far as signing big-money deals to secure branded movie slots. This helps to bring familiar cinematic action to your screen, which is designed to keep you in the game and to keep you spending your money. The enticing thing about features of new online slots is that once you activate the free spins round or a combination of wild symbols then the chances of winning bigger prizes becomes a lot greater, especially with those games that come with big multipliers.
Hit Frequency
The hit frequency is a percentage value based on how often, on average, a winning combination of symbols will appear when playing the slot. Similar to the old-style slot machines that used drum length to control the hit frequency. Game designers are now using arrays and repeat symbols for each of the drums to gain the same effect. A game with a low hit frequency is often considered as “tight” and does not produce many winning opportunities, and new online slots with a high hit frequency can often be termed as “loose” as they continue to pay out prizes, albeit a lot smaller in terms of the prizes found in the low hit frequency games.
